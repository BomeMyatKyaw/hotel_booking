Ngwe Saung Beach-Myanmar



Palm Beach Resort is a small beach front hotel which is well located on Ngwe Saung Beach. It is a quiet, laidback hotel surrounded by groves of palm trees, and manages to be rustic and luxurious at the same time.

Hotel room

There are 31 spacious and airy rooms and suites linked to the pool, restaurant, and beach by walkways. Decorated with rattan and cotton furnishings, and with either garden or sea views, accommodation at this hotel blends with the natural surroundings. Marble bathrooms, air-condition, satellite- TV and excellent facilities are provided without compromising the natural environment.
