Chaung Tha Beach-Myanmar

Chaung Tha Beach, located on Myanmar’s west coast in the Ayeyarwady Region, is a laid-back and popular destination known for its golden sands, gentle waves, and relaxed atmosphere. Just a 5–6 hour drive from Yangon, it offers a more affordable and accessible alternative to Myanmar’s upscale beaches like Ngapali. Visitors can enjoy swimming, beachside seafood, quad biking, and boat trips to nearby islands such as Phoe Kalar. The beach is especially lively during local holidays, drawing crowds of domestic tourists. With its range of budget to mid-range accommodations, Chaung Tha is ideal for travelers seeking a casual and authentic beach experience.
